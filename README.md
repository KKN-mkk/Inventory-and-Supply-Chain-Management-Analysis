###  **PROJECT OVERVIEW**
This project provides a **COMPREHENSIVE ANALYSIS** of inventory levels and supply chain performance. The objective is to identify operational bottlenecks, optimize safety stock levels, and improve overall fulfillment efficiency. This dashboard transforms raw multi-dimensional data into **ACTIONABLE BUSINESS INTELLIGENCE**.

---

###  **KEY FEATURES**

* **INVENTORY MONITORING:** Real-time tracking of **Stock-on-Hand**, **Safety Stock**, and **Stockout Risks** to prevent lost sales.
* **SUPPLY CHAIN EFFICIENCY:** Deep-dive analysis into **Lead Times**, **Order Fulfillment Cycles**, and systemic **Shipment Delays**.
* **GEOSPATIAL ANALYSIS:** Interactive mapping of distribution networks to visualize **Regional Performance Metrics**.
* **PRODUCT PERFORMANCE:** Automated **ABC ANALYSIS** to categorize high-value inventory vs. slow-moving stock.

---

###  **TECHNICAL STACK**
